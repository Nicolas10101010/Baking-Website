Cinnamon Rolls Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough: 250 ml whole milk
25 g fresh yeast
75 g soft butter
450 g flour
50 g sugar
1 pinch of salt
For the cinnamon filling: 90 g soft butter
50 g cinnamon sugar
1 tsp cocoa powder
optional: 1 pinch of ground cardamom
Extra: 1 egg whisked with a little milk
coarse sugar (for topping)
