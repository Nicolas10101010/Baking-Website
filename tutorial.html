Tutorial

  1. First get all the Ingredients.
  2. Prepare the dough: Warm the milk and dissolve the fresh yeast. Melt the butter in the warm milk, ensuring it doesn’t get too hot.
  3. Knead the dough: Mix flour, sugar, and salt. Add the yeast mixture and buttery milk, then knead until soft and elastic.
  4. First rise: Cover the dough and let it rise for about 1 hour until it doubles in size.
  5. Roll and fill: Punch down the dough and roll it into a large rectangle. Mix butter, cinnamon sugar, and cocoa powder, then spread over the dough.
  6. Shape the rolls: Fold the dough in thirds, cut into strips, twist, and form each strip into a spiral roll.
  7. Second rise: Let the shaped rolls rise again for about 30 minutes.
  8. Bake: Brush with an egg wash, sprinkle with coarse sugar, and bake at 200°C (392°F) for 13-15 minutes until golden brown.
